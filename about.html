About Shop Savvy

Shop Savvy is a simplified, Grainger-like industrial product catalog built for SparkHacks 2026. It helps users find the right industrial parts faster by providing intuitive search, side-by-side comparison, and a clean, accessible interface.

The Problem

Users struggle to pick the right industrial product quickly because specifications are complex and comparison is tedious — especially for novice users and small-to-medium industrial companies who don't have dedicated procurement experts.

What We Built

  • Product Catalog: Browse 100+ industrial products across 24 categories sourced from a real Grainger dataset.
  • Search: Keyword search across product titles, brands, descriptions, and categories.
  • Filters & Sorting: Filter by category, brand, and price range. Sort by price or name.
  • Product Details: View full specifications with organized key-value spec tables.
  • Compare: Compare up to 4 products side-by-side with highlighted differences.
  • Quote Builder: Build a parts list, adjust quantities, and export as CSV.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks.
  • Data: Static JSON dataset preprocessed from the Kaggle Grainger Products Database (140k records).
  • Search & Filtering: Client-side, all performed in-browser with JavaScript.
  • State Management: localStorage for cart and compare lists.

Data Source

Product data comes from the Kaggle Grainger Products Database . This is a static snapshot and does not reflect real-time inventory or pricing.

Disclaimer: Always verify specifications and pricing before making purchasing decisions. This application is a demonstration and does not represent actual Grainger inventory.

How to Use

  • Search: Use the search bar on any page to find products by keyword.
  • Browse: Click a category on the home page to see products in that category.
  • Filter: On the products page, use the sidebar to filter by category, brand, or price.
  • Compare: Click the compare icon on product cards to add them to comparison (up to 4).
  • Quote List: Click the cart icon to add products to your quote list, then export as CSV.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Tab — Navigate between interactive elements.
  • Enter — Activate focused button or link.
  • Escape — Close mobile menu (when applicable).
